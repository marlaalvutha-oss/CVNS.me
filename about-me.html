About Me

Brenda Varian

Virginia Notary Public & Certified Notary Signing Agent

I became a notary in 2023, and it's something I'm incredibly proud of—the fact that my signature carries weight and helps people navigate important moments in their lives. What started in Florida has now brought me to Virginia, where I've kept my credentials current and continue to serve the Central Virginia community with the same care and attention to detail that inspired me to become a notary in the first place.

My journey to becoming a notary was influenced by my own experiences. Years ago, I had a wonderful notary in Pennsylvania—the folks at Gibbins Fast Tags—who helped me with homeschool paperwork every year. They were always so kind and helpful, and I never forgot that. Now I get to be that person for others, meeting new people, hearing their stories, and making what can be a stressful process just a little bit easier.

A Bit About Me

I'm a lifelong Mid-Atlantic resident—born in Maryland just outside DC, spent years in Pennsylvania near Philadelphia, and now I'm proud to call Appomattox home. I've spent my entire life in this region, and I love it here. The terrain and weather in Virginia remind me so much of Pennsylvania, and Appomattox holds a special place in my heart. It's one of the few Civil War sites in the Mid-Atlantic my dad never made it to, and I think he would have loved it here too.

When I'm not notarizing documents, you'll find me pursuing my many creative passions: writing, desktop publishing, painting model horses and RPG miniatures, crocheting, and homesteading. I love gardening, raising chickens, rabbits, and Bourbon Red turkeys, and making jellies, jams, and canning food. I'm an avid reader and proudly autodidactic—I believe in the power of continuous learning.

I'm also getting more involved in the Appomattox community. Every summer, I host a tie-dyeing event at our local library, and I'm always looking for ways to connect with my neighbors and give back to this wonderful town.

I look forward to serving you with integrity, professionalism, and care.
